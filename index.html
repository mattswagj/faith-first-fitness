Meet the Mascot

Say hi to Fun Freddie! 🐰

Fun Freddie is the heart of Faith First Fitness — a high-energy bunny who shows kids that getting fit is an adventure, not a chore. Through obstacle courses, the basics of sports, and even a little yoga, Freddie helps kids build fine and gross motor skills, coordination, and balance.

Wherever Freddie hops, kids learn that healthy habits, teamwork, and a faith-filled attitude make them unstoppable. His motto? "I can do it!"

  • 🤸 Motor skills
  • 🧘 Coordination & balance
  • ⚽ Sports basics
  • 😄 Confidence

Meet the Coach

Led with heart by Coach Lucas

Coach Datavious Lucas founded Faith First Fitness on a simple belief: every child deserves to feel strong, capable, and loved. As a youth development specialist turned founder, he's made it his mission to fight childhood obesity and build confidence through movement and faith.

With a team of high-energy coaches and one very bouncy bunny, Coach Lucas nurtures the physical, emotional, and spiritual health of every kid who walks through the door.
