Meet the Mascot

Say hi to Fun Freddie! 🐛

Fun Freddie is the heart of Faith First Fitness — a high-energy caterpillar who shows kids that getting fit is an adventure, not a chore. Through obstacle courses, the basics of sports, and even a little yoga, Freddie helps kids build fine and gross motor skills, coordination, and balance.

Wherever Freddie wiggles, kids learn that healthy habits, teamwork, and a faith-filled attitude make them unstoppable. His motto? "I can do it!"

Meet the Coach

Led with heart by Coach Lucas

Coach Datavious Lucas founded Faith First Fitness on a simple belief: every child deserves to feel strong, capable, and loved. As a youth development specialist turned founder, he's made it his mission to fight childhood obesity and build confidence through movement and faith.

With a team of high-energy coaches and one very wiggly caterpillar, Coach Lucas nurtures the physical, emotional, and spiritual health of every kid who walks through the door.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Have another question? Call (678) 914-7132 or send a message below.

What ages do you work with?+
Everyone from wobbly toddlers (age 1) through school-age kids (age 12). Our adaptive Exceptional Needs program welcomes children of all ages and abilities.
Where are classes and events held?+
We serve families across the Atlanta & Gwinnett County, Georgia area. Fun Freddie and Coach Lucas also bring fitness fun to schools, daycares, and community events.
Do you have programs for children with special needs?+
Yes! Our caring coaches adapt every activity so exceptional-needs kids can take part, build confidence, and have a blast right alongside everyone else.
What happens at a session?+
High-energy fun — obstacle courses, sports basics, games, and balance and coordination challenges — plus simple nutrition lessons and faith-filled encouragement that helps kids believe "I can do it!"
Is Faith First Fitness faith-based?+
Yes. We nurture each child's spiritual, emotional, and physical health, and we warmly welcome families from all backgrounds.
How do I sign my child up?+
Easy — reserve a spot on our booking page, fill out the contact form below, or call Coach Lucas at (678) 914-7132.
